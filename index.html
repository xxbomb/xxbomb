THE HISTORY OF NUCLEAR TESTING




The Trinity Test

July 16, 1945

The Trinity test marked the beginning of testing nuclear weapons. Developed in the Manhattan Project, this 20kT device was detonated in the desert of New Mexico. Since this event, over 2000 nuclear tests have been run througout the world.

First Lightning

August 29, 1949

The Russian First Lightening test, signals the end of a nuclear monopoly, and the start of an arms race.
